Announcing: the Alliance for Property and Privacy Rights Protection

Mission Statement

Our cause: the advancement of property and privacy rights for the underprivileged, and for broad public benefit, through code. PropPriv provides transparent funding to select, open-source, software/hardware projects that advance our cause.

When it comes to protecting rights and freedoms, we believe that code is much more effective than alternate methods (lawsuits, Amicus briefs, lobbyists, etc.) There are already other associations who use those methods, but we will use code.

Impetus

The Edward Snowden revelations, secret court orders, and proposed controls such as SOPA and the Burr-Feinstein Anti-Encryption Bill, have made it clear that governments and corporations cannot be blindly trusted to uphold the protection of individual autonomy and collective freedom. Those in power are actively employing legal and extralegal means to violate our liberties. It is now undeniable that governments and for-profit corporations cannot be blindly trusted to protect individual autonomy and freedom. The threat of force leaves all of us open to backdoors, key theft, and the impersonation of trusted entities. The use of cryptography, combined with publicly transparent development and review, has the best chance of protecting our basic human rights, and the rights of every generation that follows.
